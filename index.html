About me

Hi, I’m Wilson Eduardo, a dedicated web developer passionate about building modern, efficient, and user-friendly digital solutions. I specialize in crafting responsive interfaces with React, TypeScript, and Tailwind CSS, ensuring both performance and great user experience.

On the backend, I work with Node.js and frameworks like Express and Fastify to create scalable APIs and robust application architectures. For data handling, I rely on tools such as Sequelize, Drizzle, and MySQL, making sure projects are built on solid and maintainable foundations.

I’m always exploring new technologies and best practices, aiming to deliver solutions that combine clean design, scalability, and real-world impact. My goal is to transform ideas into digital products.
